About Flick Bus

Welcome to Flick Bus, your reliable partner for navigating the bus routes in Kerala. Our mission is to provide commuters with easy and quick access to bus timing information, making your daily travel smoother and more predictable.

We understand the importance of timely information when it comes to public transport. Flick Bus aims to simplify your journey planning by offering up-to-date schedules and helping you find the next available bus for your route.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for bus timing information in Kerala, empowering commuters with accurate and accessible data for a hassle-free travel experience.

How it Works (Currently Simulated)

Currently, Flick Bus uses simulated data to demonstrate how a real bus timing app would function. In a fully operational version, we would integrate with reliable data sources to provide real-time or scheduled timings for various bus routes across Kerala.
