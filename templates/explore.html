Explore Destinations

Discover notable tourist destinations accessible via the bus routes covered by Flick Bus.

Near Edappal & Kuttippuram

  • Biyyam Kayal (Edappal) - Scenic backwaters
  • Thunjan Parambu (Tirur, near Kuttippuram) - Memorial for Thunchaththu Ezhuthachan
  • Bharathappuzha River - Offers scenic views
  • Various temples and mosques in the vicinity

Near Ponnani

  • Ponnani Beach - Popular spot for sunsets
  • Ponnani Juma Masjid - Historical mosque
  • Padinjarekkara Beach (near Ponnani) - Estuary where Bharathappuzha meets the Arabian Sea

Near Pattambi

  • Bharathappuzha River banks
  • Local cultural centers and temples
  • Gateway to explore Palakkad district's attractions

Near Changaramkulam

  • Markets and local town attractions
  • Proximity to coastal areas and backwaters
  • Access to routes towards Thrissur district
